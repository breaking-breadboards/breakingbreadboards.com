A meetup for electronics enthuisasts

Breaking Breadboards is a welcoming group of tech tinkerers in Brighton (UK) interested in the intersection of hardware and software, no matter their experience level.

At Breaking Breadboards meetups, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, share your projects, and learn from others. Whether you're interested in diving into the intricacies of microcontollers, exploring the latest in hardware, or simply looking for inspiration for your next project, you'll find it here.
